[5/18/23]

Alive 2020


  • artist: Skylar Spence
  • release year: 2020
  • score: 9/10

mixtape that came with the Hit Vibes 2020 repress. my true introduction to Skylar Spence/Saint Pepsi. showcases his raw talent and sound. mixes up some familiar classics, recent hits, prior Saint Pepsi songs and previously unreleased Skylar Spence demos. At one point he mixes Daft Punk with Hall & Oates. i highly recommend this one.
