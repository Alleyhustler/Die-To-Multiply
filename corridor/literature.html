My Lesbian Experience With Loneliness


  • author: Nagata Kabi
  • type: manga
  • score: 8/10
  • tw//: talks of/depicts self-harm, eating disorder stuff briefly. nothing really graphic.

a touching autobiography with an adorable art style and lots of humor. the focal point of this manga is clearly the mangaka's struggle with depression and loneliness, but her sexual orientation serves as a very important backdrop. i felt myself often empathizing with the mangaka's insecurities about her own worth and value to others. i feel like i took a few things away from this. i'm definitely checking out some of her other works.
